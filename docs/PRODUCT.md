## Vision

CampusFin AI is an **AI Operating System for campus-area micro businesses**.

We help small business owners near universities make better decisions in less time — without becoming accountants, data analysts, or power users of complex software.

> **North star:** A business owner opens CampusFin AI, understands how their business is doing in under 3 seconds, and knows exactly what to do next.

---

## Positioning

| Dimension | CampusFin AI |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Category** | Business intelligence + operational OS for campus micro businesses |
| **Primary experience** | Visual dashboards, structured reports, actionable recommendations |
| **AI role** | Decision support — not the product itself |
| **Daily usage** | Under 5 minutes per day |
| **Audience** | Owner-operators of campus-area shops (not students, not enterprise) |

CampusFin AI is **not a chatbot**. It is a **dashboard-driven SaaS platform** where AI powers insights behind the scenes.

## Core Value Proposition

Business owners use CampusFin AI to understand:

- **Business health** — Are we doing okay today?
- **Cash flow** — Can we cover this week?
- **Customer traffic** — Are enough people coming in?
- **Campus events** — What's happening nearby that affects demand?
- **Operational priorities** — What should I focus on right now?

Every insight must be **actionable** — not just informative.

---

## Product Principles

1. **AI supports business decisions** — recommendations tie to revenue, cost, or risk.
2. **Business data > conversations** — dashboards and reports are the primary interface.
3. **Under 5 minutes per day** — respect the owner's time.
4. **Every recommendation is actionable** — include a clear next step.
5. **Premium, calm UI** — Linear / Stripe / Notion / Apple — never ChatGPT.

---

## What We Are Not

- ❌ A generic AI chatbot for small business
- ❌ A personal finance app for students
- ❌ An enterprise ERP or POS replacement (at MVP stage)
- ❌ A feature factory — we optimize for paid value, not code volume
